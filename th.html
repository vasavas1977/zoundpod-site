Questions, answered

The quiet, explained.

What is a ZOUND soundproof office pod?

A ZOUND pod is a freestanding acoustic room — a soundproof booth that drops into any open office. The VOGUE Series isolates up to 32 dB, so calls, video meetings and deep work stay private without building walls. Each pod is finished like fine furniture and installs in about 45 minutes.

How quiet is a ZOUND pod?

The VOGUE Series reduces noise by up to 32 dB — roughly the difference between a busy open office and a quiet library, measured to acoustic standards (STC ≈ 30, reverberation ≈ 0.25 s). Inside, conversations stay private and the room outside falls away.

What sizes are available?

Five sizes — S, S Plus, M, L and L Plus — from a single-person focus booth to a four-person meeting room. One standard of quiet across the range; you choose the footprint.

Does the pod have ventilation and fresh air?

Yes. Every pod runs a Panasonic-developed centrifugal fan that draws fresh air in at the ceiling and exhausts it at the floor — up to 95 m³/h and under 40 dB, so the air stays fresh while the pod stays silent.

Are ZOUND pods sustainable and certified?

Yes. Pods are built with GREENGUARD Gold (UL 2818) certified materials and recycled carbon fibre, tested to ISO, SGS and ASTM standards — low-emission and built to last.
